Welcome to O.B. Montessori Center!

Thank you for your interest in enrolling your child at O.B. Montessori Center. To make the process easier for parents and guardians, our enrollment application is now done online.

Steps for Enrollment:
• Complete the online enrollment form with correct information.
• Review the list of requirements provided for submission.
• Upload the required documents and click the submit button.
• An SMS confirmation with your application ID will be sent.
• Please wait for the Admissions Office to review your application.

We look forward to welcoming you to the O.B. Montessori Center community.
